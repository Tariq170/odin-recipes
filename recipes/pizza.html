pizza

Description

Pizza is a famous Italian dish which features cooked doigh topped with fresh tomatoe sauce and plenty toppings of your choice.

This recipe shows how to make a classic homemade pizza from scratch, combining simple ingredients for amazing flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F / 45°C)
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix and knead dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  6. Roll out dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and place on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  7. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  10. Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy!
